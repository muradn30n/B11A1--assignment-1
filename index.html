Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Innovation fuels Bangladesh 2.0 by introducing new ideas and technologies. It drives growth and improves lives.

Sustainability

Sustainability in Bangladesh 2.0 ensures responsible use of resources for future generations. It promotes long-term growth and environmental care.

Infrastructure

Infrastructure in Bangladesh 2.0 focuses on modernizing facilities and connectivity. It supports economic growth and improves daily life.

Empowerment

Empowerment for Pillars of Bangladesh 2.0 strengthens key sectors, driving growth and equal opportunities for all.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

When we want to help the poor, we usually offer them charity. Yet, charity is no solution to poverty; it only perpetuates poverty by fostering dependency. It takes away the initiative from people, reducing them to beggars. Instead, we should focus on empowering the poor by providing them with the tools and opportunities they need to stand on their own feet, unleashing their potential and creativity through initiatives like microcredit and social business. This way, we can create a world where poverty exists only in museums.

Dr.Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
